Kubmo

Technology workshops for women in the developing world.

Kubmo works with global communities and programs to inspire women to pursue careers in technology and entrepreneurship. Please reach out if you're interested in a partnership!

The BizTech Workshop

We partnered with the Muthaa Community Development Foundation for the first workshop in Nairobi, Kenya. Each woman in the program applied with a business idea. Once they were accepted, they recieved a smart phone, took part in the 3-day workshop, and given a year of mentorship from mentors in their local community.
